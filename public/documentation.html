Bookings Section

In this section you have a previewed window showing all therapists who are added within the spa & all the available times they can be booked on.



Book OFFs

User may want to book a particular therapist Off

by clicking on the name of the therapist to book him/her off for the day.



Therapist who have been booked off will be color-coded & no work can be allocated under them for the day their booked off. Their allocated time frames will be grayed-out & their table allocation will be non-responsive (non-clickable) & no booking will be allocated to such a therapist. See Khethi Kunutu below.



Book INs

Re-booking the therapist In again for that day equally simple, click on his/her name, then book In, simple as that.



New Client Booking

The core, the very heart & the soul of this invention is this section, the Bookings Section. There are a lot of functionalities that make up what this system represents but in it's own, in it's very heart, what defines it is this section.

The Bookings Section makes it possible for the user of this system to be on a call with a client & book him/her for a treatment, be it a new or existing client, be it a therapist of his/her choice or at random, the aim is to make everyone's life easier. A new Client makes a booking?



As you can see, our new client Mr Soso Charmi made a new booking of the above reflected treatment, for the amount shown, on the chosen date, under his therapist of his choice. The nice thing about this is that after this booking Mr Soso Charmi is no longer a new client in this organization, like many other organizations, the aim is to celebrate the new & acknowledge the existing. His information is saved on the system, however, it is incomplete.

A reminder on the system will always reflect (in a box on the far right next to the bookings table, under Incomplete Profiles) so that during Mr Soso Charmi's appointment, when he actually comes in person all the required information can be acquired.



To fill in the rest of our client's information takes just a simple click on his name under the Incomplete Profiles section



Existing Client Booking

Existing client's data should always be kept at all times, this allows business to do an effective CRM, birthday wishes, get a geographical aspect of their customers, sexual orientation, services they use, what marketing campaign they respond to, are they satisfied, e.t.c .An Existing customer calling to make a booking should atleast be as easy searching by typing his/her name, selecting him/her on the list & making a booking, Yes?



Combo Booking

A client might like to compile a booking of her own, design what she wants & pick treatments of her choice, the system allows the user to create what is refered to as a "Combo". All the treatments the client chooses is added into a list, the amount is pre-calculated and the time is adjusted & allocated based on all treatments on the list.



Cancellations

Cancellation of any booking is as easy as booking a therapist off, click on the client's booking in the booking table, then cancel Booking.



however, there might be an important question which made a client cancel his treatment, maybe he really didn't want to cancel but he wanted to reshedule, maybe something very important came up which clashed with his/her booking, there could be one of a million things. Just like the Incomplete Profiles section, the system provides you with the Cancellation Section just above the Incomplete Profiles on the far right corner of the system.



When accessed this allows you to have the client's information on display, you can either choose to call or email the client for follow-up. This depends on the information the client has supplied.



Booking Amendment

Should a client require to ammend a booking, change a date/time slot, reallocate a therapist of his/her own choice, choose a new treatment or add to an existing treatment on the booking, The system enables the user to perform such a task with no or little hassles. Upon clicking the client's booking on the booking table, on the Edit Booking section the user can easily alter a booking.



Therapist ratings

A therapist can be rated upon which a treatment is over, a rating cannot be made prior or in the middle of a treatment, Only when the treatment is over & the user clicks on the said booking in the booking table.

A rating done on a therapist will automatically change the color of such booking in the booking table from it's original red color to a default normal black color.
